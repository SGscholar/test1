About CoachCarts Lab

CoachCarts Lab is a nonprofit chess organization dedicated to making high-quality chess education accessible to everyone, regardless of financial circumstances.

Our mission is to prove that players do not need expensive coaching or paid programs to improve at chess. Through free lessons, tournaments, study materials, mentorship, and community support, we strive to create a welcoming environment where players of all skill levels can learn, improve, and connect with others who share a passion for chess.

Since our founding, CoachCarts Lab has directly impacted over 400 members of the chess community and continues to grow toward becoming one of the world's most beneficial free chess organizations.

One of our flagship initiatives is our set of free weekly lessons, hosted live through our Discord server and open to everyone regardless of rating or experience.

Our Mission

To provide free chess education, resources, tournaments, mentorship, and community support to players around the world while promoting learning, growth, sportsmanship, accessibility, and lifelong improvement.

Academy-Style Learning Path

One of CoachCarts Lab's long-term goals is to create an organized, academy-style learning system where players progress step by step through increasingly advanced concepts.


Beginner

Learn chess fundamentals, tactical patterns, opening principles, and essential endgames while building confidence over the board.

Intermediate

Develop stronger calculation, positional understanding, strategic planning, and consistent tournament performance.

Advanced

Master opening preparation, deep analysis, practical decision making, and high-level tournament strategy.

Book a Private Lesson

As the academy develops, players will be able to track their progress, identify areas for improvement, and gradually advance through each level. Experienced members will eventually have opportunities to mentor newer players and give back to the community, creating a sustainable cycle of learning and teaching.
