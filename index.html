About CoachCarts Lab

CoachCarts Lab is a nonprofit chess organization focused on free chess education.

Our mission is to prove that improvement does not require paid coaching. Players grow through structured learning, community support, and consistent practice.

We have impacted over 400+ members through lessons, tournaments, and educational content.

Weekly live lessons are hosted on Discord and open to all skill levels.

Our Mission

To provide free chess education, tournaments, mentorship, and resources while building a global learning community.
